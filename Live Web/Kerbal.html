This website serves as a front end GUI for a database consisting of data pulled from the Kerbal Space Program video game. In the game a race of 'aliens' called Kerbals develop a space program, build ships, and explore their solar system. This database houses 5 entities. 4 basic entities are Kerbals(the astronauts), Ships, Planets and Moons. The 5th entity is Missions, which can be joined to each of the other 4 creating a complex interconnectivity.
